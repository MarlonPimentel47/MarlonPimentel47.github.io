Marlon G. Pimentel is an incoming Cloud Technology Resident at Google. Prior to joining Google, Marlon interned at Bloomberg LP in the Operations department and at IBM as a full-stack software developer.

In his senior year of high school, Marlon was named a Bloomberg Technology Scholar, bringing along a $40,000 scholarship. After graduating as Salutorian of his class, Marlon attended Binghamton University where he majored in Mathematics. At Binghamton, Marlon took computer science courses and gained most of his software knowledge from reading online books and building applications. In 2019, Marlon received an internship offer from IBM which officially began his career in technology.

Marlon grew up in the Bronx, New York where he attended the Bronx School for Law, Government and Justice. His biggest inspirations are his parents who have showed him what kindness and hard-work looks like and where it can take you. Other inspirations include Ryan Serhant, Nipsey Hussle, Chamath Palihapitiya, B-boy Ynot and Scooter Braun. Marlon is currently a tutor for Tier NYC.
